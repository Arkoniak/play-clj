(soft-body info & options)

Returns a body based on btSoftBody.

Source
(defn soft-body*
  [info]
  (assoc (Body3D. (btSoftBody. info))
         :info info))
(defmacro soft-body
  [info & options]
  `(let [body# (soft-body* ~info)
         ^btSoftBody object# (:object body#)]
     (u/calls! object# ~@options)
     body#))
