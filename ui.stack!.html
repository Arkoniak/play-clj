(stack! entity k & options)

Calls a single method on a stack.

Options
:add^Actor actor
:get-max-height
:get-max-width
:get-min-height
:get-min-width
:get-pref-height
:get-pref-width
:invalidate
:layout
Source
(defmacro stack!
  [entity k & options]
  `(u/call! ^Stack (u/get-obj ~entity :object) ~k ~@options))
