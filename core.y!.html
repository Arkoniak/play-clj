(y! object y-val)

Sets only the y position of object. If object is a screen, the y position of the camera will be set.

Source
(defn y!
  [object y-val]
  (cond
    (instance? Vector2 object) (let [^Vector2 v object]
                                   (.set v (. v x) y-val))
    (instance? Vector3 object) (let [^Vector3 v object]
                                   (.set v (. v x) y-val (. v z)))
    :else (let [^Camera camera (u/get-obj object :camera)]
            (set! (. (. camera position) y) y-val)
            (.update camera))))
